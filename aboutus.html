About Us

Our Mission is your Supply Chain

Clearflow Logistics is a third-party process management company with over 150 years of combined transpotation-management experience. We are focused on process-improvement modeling and the logistics functions that support it. We manage this process with state-of-the-art on-line transportation management systems and optimization suites. Our network connectivity with our carrier groups is second to none with hundreds of active carriers, freight-forwarders and warehouse and distribution networks participating. Clearflow understands the short comings within the supply chain and the challenges that logistics managers face on a day-to-day basis. To address these challenges we offer a comprehensive managed-freight program. A program that provides evaluated and proven supply chain and logistics services combined with the appropriate, complimentary, technology tools that leverage the Internet as a collaborative mechanism.

Our Services

Transportation Services

Full-Truckload, Partial Truckload, LTL, Rail, air/expedites, ocean and warehousing, Clearflow can ship your freight anywhere in the world.

3PL Solutions

Order Management and Fulfillment, Inbound Process Management and 3PL Process Consulting. Clearflow Logistics can analyze your needs and custom-build a solution for your shipping needs.

Final Mile Services

White-Glove, Inside deliveries, Storage and Distribution, In-Transit merge, our network of warehouse and final mile agents can perform all your final-mile needs in any of the 50 states and Canada.

Specialized Services

From standard vans, 53-foot flat-beds and conestogas, climate-control trailers and scientific-sample refrigerated trucks all the way down to express sprinter vans, Clearflow has the experience and carriers to move your freight.
